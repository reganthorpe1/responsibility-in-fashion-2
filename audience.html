Audience and Goals

Defining the target audience helps to create specific site goals for those people.

Audience

After writing all of the direct and indirect audience of Responsibility in Fashion, I ended up organizing them into three main groups: need help, give help, and skeptics. Under the "Give Help" category, I organized it into three sub-groups: volunteer, corporate sponsorship, and donate. These three sub-groups are based off of the Responsibility in Fashion website under the "Get Involved" tab, which indicates three buttons for volunteer, corporate sponsorship, and donate. Under these groups I brainstormed the target audience that would take the next step in each of these categories.

Overarching Goals

What are the goals?

Client

-Get new visitors to the site
-Inspire visitors to shop ethically
-Inspire visitors about the fast fashion problem
-Get donations to help exploited workers who have been affected by human trafficking due to fast fashion
-Connect with other organizations and companies

Audience

-To be educated about fashion sustainability
-To be inspired to change their purchase choices
-To get involved in spreading the word
-To make a difference
-Connect with RIF to volunteer

Website

-Get more traffic
-Get more clicks to the page about more information on sustainability
-Receive more donations
-To make a difference
-Connect with other organizations and companies
